Our robot works with 3 cc motors, a servo motor and a temperature meter.
the DC motors are for traction and the servo for the steering trying to imitate the steering of a real car. With the sensor we measure the temperature and when it marks more than 25 degrees it would stop the DC motors. The address is controlled by an app with the mobile by bluetooth.
As an innovation it would be to put a camera to the robot / car to be able to control it by the app more comfortably.
The design of the steering is based on that of a real car to have better handling and better control over the robot
